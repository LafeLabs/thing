Quantum Art

There are two kinds of quantum art. One is the creation of art in quantum systems, and the other is creation of classical art which expresses the quantum nature of the Universe.

This page is concerned with both. The Geometron language can be used to express many types of human-readable information in the state of a quantum information processor. That information can be allowed to evolve, then a measurement can transform it back into human readable artistic information. A web-based interface can be used to transform art to quantum states and quantum states back to art. Also, we explore the ideas of quantum mechanics with the web-based quantum duality app.
